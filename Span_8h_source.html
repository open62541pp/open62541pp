16 * View to a contiguous sequence of objects, similar to `std::span` in C++20.
17 *
18 * Spans are used to pass and return open62541 arrays without copy and to use them with the standard
19 * library algorithms. The view just holds two members: the pointer to `T` and the size, so it's
20 * lightweight and trivially copyable.
